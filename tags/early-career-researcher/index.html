    I’m writing this post as I’m cramming to finish this site before this year ends. To start this blog, I thought I could share one of my first milestones as an early career researcher–my first international conference presentation! This is me presenting our work entitled “The Influence of Warmer Sea Surface Temperatures on Southwest Monsoon Rainfall in the Philippines” in the Climate Change, Monsoon and Extreme Weather Events session (AS-03-26) of the Asia-Oceania Geosciences Society (AOGS) 11th Annual Meeting in Sapporo, Japan last July 29, 2014.
