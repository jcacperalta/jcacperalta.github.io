TWICE Line Successions (2015-2023)

The visualization shows how many times a TWICE member lyric succeeeds another member in their Korean singles. Successions are counted irrespective of order (e.g. a Jihyo-Nayeon succession is equivalent to a Nayeon-Jihyo succession). The thicker a line, the more often the succession pair happened.

The songs considered for this analysis are: Like Ooh Ahh (2015), Cheer Up (2016), TT (2016), Heart Shaker (2017), Knock Knock (2017), Likey (2017), Signal (2017), Dance The Night Away (2018), What Is Love (2018), Yes or Yes (2018), The Best Thing I Ever Did (2018), Fancy (2019), Feel Special (2019), More & More (2020), I Can't Stop Me (2020), Cry For Me (2020), Alcohol Free (2021), Scientist (2021), Talk That Talk (2022) and Set Me Free (2023)
